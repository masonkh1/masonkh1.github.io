About Me

Mason Hall has many interests and hobbies. In particular he enjoys art and other creative activities. In his spare time he likes to draw, paint, craft, or in general create.

Mason Hall is a professional illustrator and fine artist based in Utah. He specializes in both digital and oil paintings. He especially loves to depict fantasy art, landscapes, and commissioned portraits and seeks to constantly self-improve.

Mason Hall was inspired by many artists throughout his life, taking inspiration from everything such as classical artists like Rembrandt, Vermeer, to modern artists such as Rick Graham, Rob Adamson, and many others. He has taken classes from several institutions including Brigham Young University Idaho, Brigham Young University Provo, Utah Valley University, and SLCC before getting his Associates of Illustration. As a professional he continues to constantly study and improve his craft.
